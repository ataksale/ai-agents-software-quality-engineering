# Future of Quality Engineering

The future of software testing will be driven by collaborative ecosystems of AI Agents.

## Emerging Trends

### Agent Swarms

Multiple specialized agents working together.

### Autonomous Quality Platforms

End-to-end AI-driven quality management.

### Self-Healing Test Suites

Automatic adaptation to application changes.

### Continuous Risk Assessment

Real-time quality intelligence.

### Predictive Defect Prevention

Preventing issues before development is complete.

## Vision for 2030

Quality Engineering teams will increasingly focus on:

* Strategy
* Governance
* Risk Management
* Business Outcomes

while AI Agents handle repetitive operational tasks.
